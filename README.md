
# A mod that changes balance of a mod "Mini Trains" and vanilla train in many ways

> "Coded with 100% flesh, blood and little bit of tears!"

* *Every change made is optional and can be turned off with startup mod settings!!*

* ***Space age*** patch in future, but this mod is still compatible to run it with

* If You have **ANY** requests or found some bugs please report them on the my [<ins>**Factorio**</ins>](https://mods.factorio.com/mod/Mini_Trains_Balance-Rework) modpage or [<ins>**GitHub**</ins>](https://github.com/MrKustyKrab/Mini-Train-Balance-Rework/tree/master) repo


* This mod ***shouldn't*** cause anny issues when added to an existing save, though any researched that this mod changed will still be researched even if moved further in the tree

## List of changes that the mod does sorted by in game settings:
### Mini train speed and power increase:  
**Changes the acceleration and max speed and other hidden values that change the speed to be have slighly smaller top speed of the vanilla train but faster acceleration than it:**  
* max speed on paper: `270 km/h` from `173 km/h ` 
* max speed in reality (*on solid fuel while being double headed with 2 wagons or one loco with 4 wagons*) `~225 km/h`, original mini train reached `77 km/h` with this setup  
* added a 2nd slot for fuel  
* breaking force set to `7`, was `6` ~~I think~~
* power consumption set to `600KW` (*with 80% efficiency this means thay true power of the train is `480 KW`*)  
### Mini Train recipe change:  
**Changes recipe of the mini train from:**  
``15x steel plate, 10x  electrnic (green) circuits, 5x engine unit``  
to:  
``20x steel plate, 10x electrnic (green) circuits, 10x engine unit``  
### Mini Train research change:  
**Changes the research to after selected research or doesn't if selected "`Don't change`" option**  
### Mini cargo wagon recipe change:  
**Changes recipie of the mini cargo wagon from:**  
``10x iron plates, 10x steel plates, 10 iron gears``  
**to:**  
``10x iron plates, 10x steel plates, 6x iron gears, 4x iron chests``  
### Mini cargo wagon capacity buff:  
**Buffs the inventory space from `20` to `30`** (*can store now 1.5k iron ore*)  
### Mini fluid wagon recipe change:  
**Changes recipe of the mini fluid wagon from:**  
``10x steel plates, 5x iron gears, 20x pipes``  
**to:**  
``10x steel plates, 6x iron gears, 1x storage tank (fluid tank), 20x pipes``  
### Mini fluid wagon capacity buff:  
**Buffs the capacity from `30k` to `37.5k`**  
### Vanilla train speed and power increase:  
**Buffs the speed and acceleration and other hidden values and power consumption to:**  
* max speed on paper: `432 km/h`  
* max speed in reality: `~444 km/h ` (*on solid fuel while being double headed with 2 wagons or one loco with 4 wagons*) 
* braking power nerfed from `7` to `5`  
* power consumption set to `1.2MW`  
* added a 3rd slot for fuel  
### Vanilla train recipe change: 
> new setting will be added if I'll finnish the patch for space age

* **Changes the recipe from ("Don't change" setting):**  
  * `30x steel plates, 10x electrnic <sup>(green)</sup> circuits, 20x engines`  
* **if set to "Changed for base game balance":**  
  * ``2x mini locomotive, 2x speed module, 2x steam engine``  
### Vanilla train research change: 
> new setting will be added if I'll finnish the patch for space age

**Sets the required research for vanilla trains to:**  
* **if set to** `Don't change`**:**  
    *  Just like in vanilla Factorio  
*  **if set to** `To after logistics 3`**:**  
    *   Logistics 3 and if ***`vanilla train recipie`*** setting is set to `Don't change` then also  speed modules   
*  **if set to** `To after automation 3`**:**  
    *   automation 3, *like what am i supposed to say?*  
### Vanilla cargo wagon capacity buff:  
**Buffs the cargo space from `40` to `60` slots** (*can store now 3k ore*)  
### Vanilla cargo wagon recipe change:  
**Changes the recipe from:**  
``20x iron plates, 20x steel plates, 10x iron gears``  
**to:**  
``4x steel chests, 16x steel plates, 12 iron gears, 20 iron plates``  
### Vanilla fluid wagon capacity buff:  
**Changes the capactiy from `50k` to `75K`**  
### Vanilla fluid wagon recipe change  
**Changes the recipie for fluid wagon from:**  
``16x steel plates, 10x iron gears, 1x storage tank (fluid tank), 8x pipes``  
**to:**  
``16x steel plates, 12x iron gears, 2x storage tank (fluid tank), 30x pipes``
